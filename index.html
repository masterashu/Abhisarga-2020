ABOUT

Abhisarga is the annual techno-cultural festival of Indian Institute of Information Technology, Sri City. It promotes liberty, energy, enthusiasm and provides students with the opportunity to explore and rediscover themselves, by providing a platform to showcase their talent. Students from esteemed institutes all over the country are invited. The buzzing campus drives the students to reach their full artistic potential in an engaging and accessible environment.
